```html{2}
<amplify-authenticator>
  <ng-template amplifySlot="confirm-sign-up-form-fields">
    <div>Please contact the adminstrator of your account to continue!</div>
  </ng-template>

  <ng-template
    amplifySlot="authenticated"
    let-user="user"
    let-signOut="signOut"
  >
    <h2>Welcome, {{ user.username }}!</h2>
    <button (click)="signOut()">Sign Out</button>
  </ng-template>
</amplify-authenticator>
```

Additionally, you can further customize the page by updating the [headers and footers](http://localhost:5000/components/authenticator?platform=angular#headers--footers).
